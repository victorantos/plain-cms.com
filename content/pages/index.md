This is your new site. It is already live, already fast, and already yours — every word of it lives in plain files you control.

A few good places to start:

- Read the [blog](/blog/) to see what posts look like.
- Open the [About](/about/) page and replace it with your own story.
- When the admin panel arrives, you'll edit everything from your browser — until then, every page is a Markdown file in `content/`.

Nothing you do here can be lost. Every change is saved in history, and any version can be restored.
