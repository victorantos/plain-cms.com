The fastest way to feel at home here is to change three things.

## 1. The name

Open `site.config.json` and change the title and description. They appear in the header, the browser tab, search results, and the feed.

## 2. The words

Rewrite [About](/about/) and [Contact](/contact/) — they are placeholders on purpose. Then write one real post and delete the examples (each one says `example: true` at the top, so they're easy to find).

## 3. The look

The theme's colors, fonts, and spacing are all defined as tokens at the top of `themes/default/assets/theme.css`. Change `--color-accent` and watch the whole site follow. Deeper restyling — and switching between ready-made themes — gets its own screen in the admin panel later.

That's it. Three changes, and it stops being an example and starts being a website.
